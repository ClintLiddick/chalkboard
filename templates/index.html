What is Chalkboard?

Chalkboard allows streamlined communication between Instructors and Students. Whereas other large systems require dedicated administrators and seem to make life harder, Chalkboard is simple.

Instructors can simply Login with their Google Account and easily begin creating class pages. You will seamlessly be able to import student lists, set up meeting times, post solution guides and announcements, and more -- all from a simple dashboard.
