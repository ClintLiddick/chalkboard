What is Chalkboard?

Chalkboard allows streamlined communication between Instructors and Students. Whereas other large systems require dedicated administrators and seem to make life harder, Chalkboard is simple.

Simply Login with your Google Account, set your school email, and create a class page. Then you're all set to import a student list, setup meeting times, post examples and announcements, and manage class notes and presentations, all from a simple dashboard.

Instructors
The instructor role enables the user to create a class page, upload documents, manage calendar information,set visibility of documents, assign grades, and invite students to join a class.
Students
The student role enables the user to join/view class pages, upload documents, and view calendar info.
